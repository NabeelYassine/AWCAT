Book Overview

Manual of the Warrior of Light is an invitation to each of us to live our dream, to embrace the uncertainty of life, and to meet our own unique destiny. In his inimitable style, Paulo Coelho helps us discover the warrior of light within each of us.

With inspiring short passages, we are invited to embark upon the way of the warrior: the one who appreciates the miracle of being alive, the one who accepts failure and the one whose quest leads him to become the person he wants to be.

Paulo Coelho is one of the most widely read authors in the world today. Renowned for his best-loved work The Alchemist , which has sold over 150 million copies worldwide, won 115 international prizes and awards, has been translated into 80 languages, and is still on the New York Times bestseller list today.
